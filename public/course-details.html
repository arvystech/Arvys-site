Course Description

This comprehensive bootcamp is designed to take you from a complete beginner to a job-ready full stack developer. You will learn the industry-standard MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) through hands-on projects and real-world scenarios.

Whether you want to work as a freelancer, join a top tech company, or build your own startup, this course provides the solid foundation and advanced skills you need to succeed in the modern tech landscape.

Requirements
A computer with internet connection (Windows, Mac, or Linux)
Basic understanding of how websites work (helpful but not required)
Passion to learn and build amazing web applications
Commitment of 10-15 hours per week for 12 weeks
